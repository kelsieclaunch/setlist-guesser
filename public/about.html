How to Play

• I didn't want people to have to connect personal information, so it's not gonna ask for your name or email, which also means there's no “forgot password” option. Remember it!!

• The forms for each show open at 9pm two days before the show. The form closes at 9pm (venue time) on the day of the show.

• Surprise songs are worth 2 points. All other questions are worth 1 point.

• The order you guess surprise songs in doesn't matter.

• In case of a tie, the user who completed their guesses first will be ranked higher.
